Strange Tarot's

  • DEATH

    XIII

    Description

    Death is the 13th trump or Major Arcana card in most traditional Tarot decks. It is used in Tarot card games as well as in divination. It is often interpreted as signifying major changes in a person's life.

  • THE SUN

    XIX

    Description

    The Sun Tarot card is the Major Arcana card of positivity, optimism, freedom and fun. When The Sun appears in an upright position, things should be going well for you as this card also represents success and enthusiasm.

  • THE WORLD

    XXI

    Description

    Assured success, recompense, voyage, route, emigration, flight, change of place. Reversed: Inertia, fixity, stagnation, permanence. The World represents an ending to a cycle of life, a pause in life before the next big cycle beginning with the fool.
